Services

What I do

I love bringing products from someone's brain to the screen. Leading a team of creatives through the process of building something beautiful is what I live for. Starting from the strategic research and brainstorming phase, merging many ideas into one great idea, forming prototypes and testing it with users and in the end building the final product; it can't get much better than that. Nevertheless projects often start to fast with have a not so smooth start which can result in a product that no one is really happy with. I believe that with the right planning, evaluation and team building during the project even the most crooked beginnings can lead to an amazing end product.

About Me

Who I am

My journey so far has taken me to loads of different places: I was born in Hamburg. Finished High School there in 2011. Moved to London to work in a Youth Retreat centre. Found my love for technology, photography and design. After one year in the UK I moved back to Hamburg. Did an Internship at SinnerSchrader (one of Europe’s leading digital agencies) in the Strategic Department. In 2013 I moved to the small city of Deventer in the Netherlands, where I started studying Human Information Design and Strategy. Now it is 2016 and after moving to a new citiy I am a student of Art & Technology at the Saxion University of Applied Science in Enschede. Here I am working with extremely creative and inspiring people on subjects such as Video Production, Graphic Design, Web Technology, Programming and Media & Communication.
